Orientation

Orientation Agenda

In the recent DevOps Cloud Computing online orientation held via Zoom, participants were introduced to the foundational principles of integrating DevOps practices with cloud computing platforms. The session covered the benefits of automating deployment, scaling, and management of applications in cloud environments. Expert speakers demonstrated tools and best practices, emphasizing the importance of collaboration between development and operations teams. Attendees were given insights into how DevOps in the cloud accelerates software delivery, improves operational efficiency, and reduces infrastructure costs. The orientation can be viewed via this youtube link.
